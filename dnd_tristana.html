Tristana's Wild Magic

An attempt to make magic come alive

This is a webpage for my Dungeon's and Dragon's Character - a girl who has wild magic she can't control, and gets less stable based on her emotional state.

Introduction

This system was created to make the game more interesting, and to help the DM guide the story, the player characters, and Tristana’s personal character development. The system serves the story, not the other way around. Feel free to fudge anything here to enrich the DnD experience. At its core, this webpage is a collection of guidelines to help you better understand and curate Tristana’s emotional and magical journey.

Who is Tristana?

  • Sentimental
  • Emotional
  • Fearful
  • Oblivious
  • Mom died during childbirth, and passed a curse on to Tristana
  • Left the civilized world at age 8
  • Grew up in the wild
  • Developing Sense of Morality
  • She's really just a normal girl with unfortunate circumstances.

Tristana's Relationship with Magic

  1. Tristana is afraid of her own magic – at a young age, she accidently killed her family.
  2. Tristana’s magic is the physical manifestation of her emotions. In moments of strong resolve, anger, fear, etc. Tristana will cast magic that is emotionally appropriate, even if she doesn’t want to.
  3. She is deathly afraid of her magic, but she isn’t bad at it. Her magic is destabilized by her fear and lack of practice.

Emotional Magic Guidelines

If Tristana is ever at all emotional, or wants something strongly, use this section instead of the general wild magic section

Whenever Tristana has a strong emotional reaction. She casts magic

These are guidelines for example reactions.

Highly emotional moments (examples):

  • Coming home and realizing her best friend was assassinated:
    • Despair – darkness. Black hole (suck in).
    • Stamina drain to those around her.
    • Crying like crazy
  • Watching the Big Bad kill her best friend in front of her:
    • Anger – flame aura.
    • All damaging spells cast become fire element.
    • Extended reach/power of spells, but only targets the Big Bad.
  • Watching her best friend getting shot in the stomach and bleed out:
    • Healing tears
  • Party falling down a pitfall to their death:
    • Panic – featherfall (or antigravity) on everyone
  • Being level 1, and seeing a lv20 dragon:
    • Fear – cast invisible on everyone (including dragon)
    • Or randomly teleport party to random hiding spots around dragon.
    • Or cast petrify on self.

Uncontrolled Wild Magic

Intent: Everyday magic casting that just fucks up sometimes. The most random, gimmicky portion of wild magic (a bit of randomness to lead to some unexpected fun situations).
